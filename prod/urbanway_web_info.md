## Our Services (Page 3) /services

```
At Urbanway Construction Management, we provide a full spectrum of construction and development services, ensuring that projects are delivered efficiently, on time, and within budget. Our expertise spans development management, construction management, and general contracting, supporting clients from initial planning to final occupancy.
```

- Development Management
```
We provide comprehensive land development planning services for property developers, private landowners, and public authorities. Our expertise covers every stage, from due diligence and approvals to zoning amendments and land acquisition analysis.

Key Services:
- Land Acquisition Due Diligence – Conducting research and analysis to support informed investment decisions.
- Zoning By-Law & Official Plan Amendments – Assisting with municipal approvals for land use adjustments.
- Site Plan Approvals & Permitting – Managing submissions and liaising with authorities to secure approvals.
- LPAT & Committee of Adjustments – Representing clients in public meetings and regulatory hearings.
- Concept Planning & Renderings – Developing visualizations and strategic plans to optimize land use.

By providing objective, professional guidance, we empower clients to make confident, well-informed development decisions.
```
- Construction Management
```
Our Construction Management division provides leadership and oversight for all aspects of the construction process. From tendering and contract administration to post-construction closeout, we ensure projects are delivered efficiently and meet all quality, safety, and budgetary goals.

Key Services:
- Project Management & Administration – Coordinating construction phases with clear oversight and documentation.
- Trade Contract Requisitions – Handling the selection and coordination of subcontractors and suppliers.
- Logistics Planning & Site Supervision – Ensuring smooth operations and compliance with site safety standards.
- Cost Control & Hard Cost Budgets – Managing finances to maximize value and minimize risk.
- Occupancy Sign-offs & Start-up Commissioning – Handling final approvals and ensuring a seamless transition to occupancy.

With proactive risk management and detailed planning, we help clients stay on schedule and on budget.
```

- General Contracting
```
Urbanway's General Contracting division delivers high-quality construction solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. We operate with a commitment to quality, efficiency, and client satisfaction, ensuring every project meets the highest industry standards.

Key Services:
- Trade Requisitions & Controlled Change Orders – Managing contracts, scope adjustments, and seamless execution.
- Site Supervision & Coordination – Overseeing daily operations to maintain efficiency and safety.
- Quality Assurance & Safety Programs – Implementing strict protocols for compliance and risk mitigation.
- Monthly Reporting & Scheduling – Keeping stakeholders informed with detailed progress updates.
- Fixed Fee & Stipulated Price Contracts – Offering flexible contract structures to fit project needs.

With a hands-on approach, we ensure that every construction project is executed with precision, cost control, and quality assurance.
```
